About the Event

Join us for a day of giving back to nature! We'll be planting 100 native trees in Central Park to help improve air quality, provide habitat for wildlife, and beautify our community. Volunteers will assist with digging holes, planting saplings, mulching, and watering. No prior experience is necessary, but a love for the outdoors is a plus! Please wear comfortable clothing and closed-toe shoes. We'll provide all necessary tools and gloves. Your participation will directly contribute to a greener, healthier future for our city.
