About Us

ImpactHub is a platform dedicated to connecting non-profit organizations, donors, and volunteers to create meaningful social impact.

Our Mission

At ImpactHub, we believe in the power of collective action. We strive to create a world where everyone has the opportunity to contribute to positive change. Our platform provides a space for non-profit organizations to showcase their projects and needs, donors to find causes they are passionate about, and volunteers to offer their time and skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

You can donate directly on any campaign page. We accept all major credit cards. All donations to registered 501(c)(3) organizations are tax-deductible.

You can find volunteer opportunities on our "Volunteer Events" page. Simply find an event that matches your interests and click "Apply" or "Register" to get started.

If you are a registered non-profit organization, you can sign up on our "Get Started" page to create a profile, post campaigns, and recruit volunteers.
